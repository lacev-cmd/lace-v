How it works

01

Your input

Set a baseline — where things stand right now. Add to it over time. The more honestly you put in, the more clearly it reads back.

02

The machine reads

It works through your material. It counts what appears independently. It finds what is consistent, what conflicts, what is building, and what is absent. It does not generate, guess, or fill gaps.

03

Your output

A map of what it found — bounded entirely by what you wrote. Every claim carries a confidence label. Nothing is invented. Nothing is added.

04

You stay in control

If the map misreads something, you correct it. Corrections govern every subsequent reading. The machine does not override you.
