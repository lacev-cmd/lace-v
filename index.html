The Pattern Reader.

Welcome to Lace V. We are here with one purpose — to try and understand. To understand where we are going, what's failing, what the outlook is, or isn't. We try to give you an extra layer of information to act upon or not. It's all in your hands. The cleaner the input, the better the tool will serve you.

What the engine does

Lace V reads your material and maps what it finds. It counts what appears independently across your entries. It tracks what is moving and what is not. It holds contradictions without resolving them. It surfaces what is absent. It reads load — what is making things harder. It detects state changes — genuine shifts versus noise. And it tells you what the next useful move is, bounded by what the evidence actually supports.

It does not generate. It does not predict. It does not guess. It reads what you give it and maps what is there.

It will only read what you input. The better the input, the better the tool will serve you.
