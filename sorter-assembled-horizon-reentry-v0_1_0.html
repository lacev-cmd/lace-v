Horizon Map

Reads where your arc is pointing. Shows the gap between that and where you said you want to go.

What this is. A planning surface built from what you write. It reads the pattern of your entries over time, projects that arc forward to a horizon you choose, and compares where it is pointing to where you said you want to be.

The gap between those two things is what you are actually planning across. It is not a forecast. It does not tell you what will happen. It is a structural picture — honest about uncertainty, updated every time you add an entry.

It faces forward. The map behind it reads the past. This surface uses that reading to project toward the future. Every projection is labelled as such. Nothing here is certain.

This is your record. Nothing leaves this device. No account. No data stored anywhere but here.
