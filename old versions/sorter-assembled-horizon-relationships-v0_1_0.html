Relationship Horizon

Reads where your relationship arc is pointing. Shows the gap between that and where you said you want it to go.

What this is. A planning surface built from what you write about a relationship. It reads the pattern of your entries over time — what you are doing toward the other person, what is moving, what is circling — and projects that arc forward to a horizon you choose.

It compares where the arc is pointing to where you said you want the relationship to go. The gap between those two things is what you are actually navigating. It is not a forecast. It does not tell you what will happen or what you should do.

It reads what is done, not just what is felt. Feelings about a relationship are real. But what you actually do toward the other person is the more reliable signal of where things are heading. The map reads both.

This is your record. Nothing leaves this device. No account. No data stored anywhere but here.
