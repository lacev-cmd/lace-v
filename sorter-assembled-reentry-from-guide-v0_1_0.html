Personal Map

A reading and sorting tool. You write. It reads what you wrote.

What this is. This map is built from what you wrote. Nothing more. It cannot see what you did not give it. It cannot fill gaps you left empty. It cannot read what you held back. The more honestly you write, the more honestly it reads.

It does not generate, predict, or guess. It reads your material, counts what appears independently, maps what is moving and what is not, holds what contradicts without resolving it, and surfaces what is missing.

This is your record. Nothing leaves this device. No account required. No data stored anywhere but here.
