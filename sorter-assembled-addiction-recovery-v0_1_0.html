Addiction Recovery Map

This tool doesn't judge where you are in your recovery. It doesn't know what you've been through and it won't tell you what to do. It reads what you write and maps what's actually there — what's moving, what's under pressure, what the pattern looks like right now.

Write honestly. That's all it needs.

HOW TO USE THIS TOOL

Write where things stand today. Come back when something shifts — a hard moment, a good one, a close call, a decision. The map reads across everything you give it.

A minimum baseline might look like this:
"I've been clean for three months. It's going okay mostly but there are days when it doesn't feel real. The people and places I need to avoid are still there. I haven't told my family how close it still feels sometimes."

Write more if you have it. That's enough if you don't.

This tool is not a substitute for professional support. It reads what you write and maps what it finds — no more. It does not diagnose, assess risk, or produce a clinical or legal finding. If you are in crisis or need immediate help, please reach out to someone who can support you directly.
